French Toast Recipe

This recipe came from my high school cooking class. I'm ripping it straight off of the old assignment sheet, but there are a few discrepancies between the ingredients and the directions, so forgive me.

Ingredients:

  • 4 thick slices of bread
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 tbsp. milk
  • 1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp. ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp. vanilla extract
  • Pancake syrup

Directions:

  1. Combine in a small bowl:
    • cinnamon
    • nutmeg
    • sugar
  2. Set it aside
  3. In a 10-inch skillet, melt butter over medium heat
  4. Whisk together the cinnamon mixture with the eggs, milk, and vanilla, and then pour into a shallow container such as a pie plate
  5. Dip the sliced of bread into the mixture
  6. Fry slices until golden brown, then flip to cook the other side
  7. Serve with warm syrup
