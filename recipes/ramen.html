Ramen Recipe

Description

Traditional, Shoyu ramen, cooked in pork neck bone broth

Ingredients

  • 6 lb Pork Neck Bone
  • 2 lb Pork Shoulder or Pork Belly
  • 1 Large Leek
  • 2 ginger roots
  • 1 dried konbu or kelp
  • 2 bulbs of garlic
  • 1 & 1/2 tbsps. chicken powder
  • 2 tbsp sake
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 & 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup sake
  • 3/4 cup mirin
  • 2 Eggs
